Part 3: Distex, Sebastian James

Essentially, my idea was to create different textures layered on top of each other.

1. Dissonance vs consonance
2. Gain
3. Density
4. Tempo

To do that, I start with a b diminished chord which is played with variant textures and tempos. Following, I used a SndBuf UGen to import a heartbeat sample which I played through the density engine created in Part 2. Additionally, I swept the LPF from high to low, so that while density continued at random, the LPF decreased linearly from 800 Hz to 50 Hz. Following the "heartbeat section", I played the chord progression from part 1 through the same signal chain as earlier in the statement, finally completing a modulation from C, to Am (I to iv).
